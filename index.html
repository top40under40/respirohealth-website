The Gap

The system can only see patients who arrive.
We are building for the ones who don't.

Most respiratory diseases go undetected because people don't seek care in time — due to normalising symptoms, stigma, distance, or not knowing their cough is serious. By the time they reach a doctor, the disease has progressed. The gap is pre-diagnostic, and it is enormous.

70M+
Indians estimated to have COPD or asthma — the majority undiagnosed or undertreated
43%
Of global asthma deaths occur in India — despite asthma being a manageable condition with early intervention
37%
Of Indian adult smokers have COPD — most don't know it until significant lung damage has occurred
The Approach

Push model vs Pull model.
A fundamental difference.

Existing AI screening tools use a push model — a trained health worker goes out to find the patient. Respiro is a pull model — the patient initiates screening themselves, privately, before they've decided to visit a clinic.

1

Patient opens WhatsApp

No app download. No registration. Works on any phone that already has WhatsApp — 500 million in India.

2

Records a cough

A 30-second guided voice note, in the patient's own language. Private. No health worker present.

3

AI analyses the recording and symptoms

Respiratory patterns combined with symptom inputs are processed by our AI pipeline. A risk assessment is generated in seconds.

4

Guided to the right care

High-risk patients are guided to the nearest appropriate healthcare facility. The system routes people toward care — not around it.
