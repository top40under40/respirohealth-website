The Problem

Every consultation starts
from a blank page.

India's OPDs see 6 billion visits a year. In the vast majority, the doctor begins with zero information about the patient sitting in front of them. No structured history. No pre-visit triage. No way to distinguish a routine case from a critical one before the patient walks in. The problem is not the consultation — it is everything that happens before it.

60%+

The Product

Pre-visit. During. Post-visit.
We own the full patient journey.

Respiro sits between appointment booking and consultation — capturing structured patient data before the visit, surfacing it to the doctor in real time, and following up automatically after. One platform. Zero disruption to existing workflows.

Pre-Visit
Intelligent Intake

Patient scans a QR code or messages the clinic on WhatsApp — no app download required

Answers structured clinical questions in their language in 3–5 minutes

Completed before the patient enters the clinic — doctor is already informed

Zero friction — works on any phone with WhatsApp already installed

During Visit
Doctor & COO Intelligence

AI-generated triage summary ready on the doctor's dashboard before the patient sits down

High-risk cases automatically flagged in the queue — critical patients never wait undetected

ICD-10 diagnosis and medications logged post-consultation in one workflow

COO dashboard shows live queue, funnel, and doctor-efficiency metrics in real time

Post-Visit
Adherence & Follow-up

Patient automatically contacted at Day 3, 7, 14, and 30 — in their language, on WhatsApp

Asked how they are feeling — structured responses captured and logged

Non-recovering patients flagged to the doctor immediately — before they deteriorate

Builds a longitudinal outcomes record per patient — automatically, over time

No change to existing infrastructure. No training required for doctors. No app download for patients. The receptionist or patient initiates — the doctor just opens the dashboard.

The Vision

Built for one clinic.
Designed for a hundred.

Respiro starts with respiratory OPDs — the highest-complexity intake environment in Indian healthcare. The same platform deploys across specialties, across hospital groups, and across geographies without rebuilding from scratch.

01

Starts with respiratory

Purpose-built for pulmonology OPDs — clinically reviewed symptom architecture, multilingual flows, and a triage model designed for India's respiratory disease burden.

02

Expands across specialties

The same infrastructure deploys to Cardiology, General Medicine, and beyond. No rebuilding. No new training. One platform, every department.

03

Grows smarter over time

Every patient interaction generates structured, diagnosis-linked clinical data. The more OPDs that run Respiro, the more accurate and clinically relevant the platform becomes.

We are not building a tool for one department. We are building the intelligence layer for the Indian OPD.
